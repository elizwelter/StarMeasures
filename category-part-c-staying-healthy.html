Staying Healthy

These measures focus on preventive care, screenings, and maintaining overall health, primarily aimed at healthy members or reducing risks for chronic conditions.

Star Measure Description
Breast Cancer Screening Ensures that women get the recommended breast cancer screening to detect cancer early.
Colorectal Cancer Screening Promotes screening for colorectal cancer in at-risk individuals to detect cancer early.
Annual Flu Vaccine Ensures that members receive their flu vaccination annually to reduce flu-related complications.
Monitoring Physical Activity Encourages members to engage in regular physical activity to maintain overall health and wellness.
Osteoporosis Management in Women Focuses on managing osteoporosis in women who have had a fracture to reduce the risk of future fractures.
Diabetes Care – Eye Exam Ensures that individuals with diabetes receive regular eye exams to prevent diabetic retinopathy.
Diabetes Care – Blood Sugar Controlled Measures how well individuals with diabetes maintain control of their blood sugar levels.
