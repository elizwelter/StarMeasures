Drug Safety and Accuracy of Pricing

These measures focus on ensuring that drugs are prescribed accurately, that medication pricing is correct, and that medications are appropriately managed, especially for chronic conditions.

Star Measure Description
MPF Price Accuracy Measures the accuracy of the pricing information for prescription drugs in the Medicare Plan Finder (MPF), ensuring beneficiaries can compare prices correctly across plans.
Medication Adherence for Diabetes Medications Assesses whether members with diabetes are adhering to prescribed medication regimens, which is critical for managing the condition effectively.
Medication Adherence for Hypertension (RAS antagonists) Measures adherence to medication for patients with hypertension, specifically those using renin-angiotensin system (RAS) antagonists, to reduce cardiovascular risks.
Medication Adherence for Cholesterol (Statins) Measures adherence to statin medications for patients with high cholesterol, which helps in reducing cardiovascular disease risks.
Statin Use in Persons with Diabetes (SUPD) Assesses whether patients with diabetes are being prescribed statin medications to help lower the risk of cardiovascular events, a common issue for diabetic patients.
