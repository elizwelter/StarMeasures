Member Complaints and Change in Plan's Performance

These measures track member complaints, plan quality improvements, and changes in plan performance, helping to assess the overall quality and response to member feedback.

Star Measure Description
Complaints about the Plan Measures the number of member complaints about the health plan, assessing the plan's responsiveness to issues raised by its members.
Members Choosing to Leave the Plan Assesses the rate at which members decide to leave the health plan, which can reflect dissatisfaction with the plan's services or coverage.
Health Plan Quality Improvement Measures the efforts and effectiveness of the health plan in improving its quality over time, based on member feedback and internal assessments.
Plan Makes Timely Decisions about Appeals Assesses whether the health plan makes timely decisions on member appeals, including coverage decisions and other administrative requests.
Reviewing Appeals Decisions Assesses the process of reviewing appeal decisions to ensure that member requests for reconsideration are handled fairly and efficiently.
