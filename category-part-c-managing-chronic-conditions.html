Managing Chronic Conditions

These measures assess how well health plans manage chronic diseases and conditions in their members, with an emphasis on effective treatment, medications, and care coordination.

Star Measure Description
Special Needs Plan (SNP) Care Management Assessment of care management for beneficiaries with special needs, ensuring they receive appropriate care and support.
Care for Older Adults – Medication Review Ensures older adults have their medications reviewed regularly to avoid adverse drug interactions and ensure proper medication management.
Care for Older Adults – Pain Assessment Assesses the effectiveness of pain management in older adults to improve quality of life.
Diabetes Care – Blood Sugar Controlled Measures the percentage of members with diabetes who have controlled blood sugar levels to prevent complications.
Controlling Blood Pressure Tracks the number of members with hypertension whose blood pressure is well-controlled.
Statin Therapy for Patients with Cardiovascular Disease Measures how many members with cardiovascular disease are appropriately prescribed statins to lower cholesterol levels.
Transitions of Care Assesses the effectiveness of care coordination when patients are transitioning from one healthcare setting to another.
Follow-up after Emergency Department Visit for People with Multiple High-Risk Chronic Conditions Measures whether members with multiple chronic conditions receive timely follow-up care after an emergency department visit.
Improving Bladder Control Tracks the effectiveness of interventions to improve bladder control in at-risk populations.
Reducing the Risk of Falling Focuses on measures to reduce fall risks in older adults and individuals with mobility challenges.
Medication Reconciliation Post-Discharge Ensures that medication records are reconciled when a patient is discharged from a hospital to avoid medication errors.
Plan All-Cause Readmissions Measures the percentage of members who are readmitted to the hospital after discharge for any cause, aiming to reduce avoidable readmissions.
