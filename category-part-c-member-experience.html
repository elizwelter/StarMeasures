Member Experience with the Health Plan

These measures focus on how members perceive their experience with the health plan, including satisfaction with healthcare quality and how easily they can access care.

Star Measure Description
Getting Needed Care Measures the ease with which members are able to get the care they need, including primary care visits and specialist referrals.
Getting Appointments and Care Quickly Assesses the timeliness with which members can get appointments and receive medical care, such as office visits and procedures.
Customer Service Assesses the quality of customer service, including how well health plan representatives address member inquiries and resolve issues.
Rating of Health Care Quality Measures members' overall satisfaction with the quality of care they receive, including both medical treatment and preventive services.
Rating of Health Plan Assesses members' overall satisfaction with their health plan, including plan features, benefits, and communication.
Care Coordination Measures how effectively the health plan coordinates care among different healthcare providers to improve member outcomes.
